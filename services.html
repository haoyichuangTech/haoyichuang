Business Introduction

Three core modes to match different product maturity stages, from outsourcing support to fully independent operation.

1. Project Co-Operation

  • Store listing strategy and launch operation
  • Content event cadence and activity design
  • Lifecycle retention and ad revenue optimization
  • Localization-driven global market management

2. Project Co-Development

  • Technical architecture and feature implementation
  • Client and backend performance engineering
  • CI/CD, release automation, and quality gates
  • Security hardening and compliance integration

3. Self-Developed Product Operation

  • In-app subscriptions and period passes
  • Consumable item charging and bundles
  • Premium function unlock and VIP tiers
  • Ad monetization across mediation networks
